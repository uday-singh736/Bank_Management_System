SBI Gold Loan Scheme
--------------------------------------------------

The State Bank of India offers secure and convenient Gold Loan services to meet your financial needs.

Key Features:
1. Loan available against pledged gold ornaments and jewellery.
2. Quick disbursal with minimal documentation.
3. Competitive interest rates tailored for different customer segments.
4. Flexible repayment options – choose EMI or bullet repayment.
5. Transparent evaluation of gold with no hidden charges.
6. Loan tenure ranging from 3 months to 36 months.
7. Facility available across most SBI branches nationwide.

Eligibility:
- Any individual above 18 years of age with valid KYC documents.
- Gold ornaments must be of acceptable purity as per SBI norms.

Benefits:
- Immediate liquidity without selling your gold.
- Safe custody of pledged gold by SBI.
- Option to top-up or renew loans easily.
