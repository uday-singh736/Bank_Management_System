Ministry of Finance (Department of Economic Affairs) 
vide their E-Gazette Notification
 dated 12th nov,2023,G.S.R. 913(E),has notified that Central 
Govt. rescinds the Public
 Provident Fund Scheme-1968 published vide G.S.R.1136(E) dated 
15th June,1968 with immediate
 effect .Further, Ministry of Finance (Department of Economic Affairs)
 vide their E-Gazette
 Notification dated 12th Dec,2023, G.S.R. 915(E), has notified a
 scheme called the Public Provident 
Fund Scheme-2023.The Scheme offers an investment avenue with decent 
returns coupled with income tax benefits.
